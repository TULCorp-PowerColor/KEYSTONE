# KEYSTONE

KEYSTONE is a powerful software solution designed to give users detailed control over their PowerColor GPU and gaming gear devices, such as mice and headsets. With KEYSTONE, you can fine-tune your hardware settings to optimize performance, personalize your gaming experience, and get the most out of your devices.

## Features

- **Comprehensive GPU Control:**  
  Adjust or monitor PowerColor GPU settings, including clock speeds, fan curves, RGB lighting, and more.

- **Gaming Gear Customization:**  
  Configure advanced settings for supported gaming mice and headsets, such as DPI, button mapping, lighting effects, and DTS audio profiles.

- **User-Friendly Interface:**  
  Intuitive and modern UI designed for both beginners and advanced users.

- **Profile Management:**  
  Save, load, and switch between multiple device profiles for different games or workflows.

## Supported Devices

- PowerColor Graphics Cards: Red Devil 6000, 7000, 9000 Series
- Alphyn Mouse 10
- Alphyn Headset 10

## Getting Started

1. **Download and Install:**  
   Download the latest version of KEYSTONE from our official website.

2. **Connect Your Devices:**  
   Plug in your PowerColor GPU and supported gaming gear.

3. **Launch KEYSTONE:**  
   Open the application and start customizing your hardware.
