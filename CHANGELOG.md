## [25.02.04] - AM10 Gaming Mouse Compatible

### What's New
AM10 has been added to Keystone Control Utility! Pair your AM10 with Keystone to elevate your gaming experience.

#### Key Features
- Customize Buttons with 3 category options: mouse functions, keyboard shortcut, and more functions.
- Macro Command
- DPI control: Customize 5 stages of DPI with maximum of 26K
- Polling Rate: Adjust polling rate for each connection (2.4, USB, Bluetooth) as you prefer.
- Battery settings: Set your mouse to sleep mode by idle time, or to eco mode by battery level. Customize battery indicators for low, medium, full power.
- RGB settings: 8 RGB patterns and hundreds of color options.
